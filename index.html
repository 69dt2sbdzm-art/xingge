📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Game Overview

"Who's the Spy?" is a fun word-guessing game where players try to identify the hidden spy among them!

👥 Roles

  • 👨‍⚖️ Judge: Knows all words, hosts the game
  • 👥 Civilians: Get the same word
  • 🕵️ Spy: Gets a different word, must hide identity

🎮 How to Play

  1. Create Room: One player creates a room and gets a 4-digit room code
  2. Join: Other players enter the room code to join
  3. Start: Judge clicks "Start Game" (needs 3+ players)
  4. Get Words: Everyone secretly views their assigned word
  5. Describe: Take turns describing your word without saying it directly
  6. Vote: Discuss and vote on who you think is the spy!

🏆 Winning

  • Civilians win if they correctly identify the spy
  • Spy wins if they fool the civilians and avoid detection

💡 Tips

  • Be creative with your descriptions!
  • Pay attention to others' clues
  • Spy: Try to blend in while being vague
  • Civilians: Look for inconsistencies
